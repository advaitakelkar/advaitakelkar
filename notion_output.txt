Intro — Column 1
This digital platform serves as the comprehensive, living archive of Advaita Kelkar's multidisciplinary design practice. It is designed to function as a singular, unified ecosystem that seamlessly integrates a diverse and expansive body of work. Here, projects spanning across architecture, interior environments, physical sculpture, graphic design, product innovation, and fine art are not treated as isolated endeavors, but rather as interconnected expressions of a singular creative vision.
[Unsupported block type: divider]
Intro — Column 2 (Read More)
Rather than compartmentalizing these various creative fields into rigid categories, this archive encourages fluid exploration. It invites visitors to navigate freely across different disciplines, uncovering the subtle threads and underlying dialogues that connect them. Through this unstructured journey, one can trace how foundational principles of form, tactile material choices, and core conceptual intentions recur, evolve, and echo from one project to the next.
